Popular Tourist Places in Vizag

RK Beach

One of the most popular beaches in Visakhapatnam, known for its golden sands and beautiful sunsets. Perfect for evening walks and water sports.

Kailasagiri

Hilltop park offering panoramic views of the city and Bay of Bengal. Features a giant Shiva-Parvati statue and various recreational activities.

Borra Caves

Ancient limestone caves with stunning stalactite and stalagmite formations. A natural wonder located in the Ananthagiri hills.

Araku Valley

Scenic hill station surrounded by coffee plantations and tribal villages. Famous for its natural beauty and cultural heritage.

Submarine Museum

INS Kursura submarine turned museum, offering a unique insight into naval history. A must-visit attraction on RK Beach.
